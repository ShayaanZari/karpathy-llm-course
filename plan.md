### Week 1
- **Linear Regression Foundations:** Mapping inputs to outputs, defining residuals, and understanding irreducible error.
- **The Cost Function:** Setting up Mean Squared Error (MSE) to quantify model performance.
- **Optimization via Gradient Descent:** Deriving partial derivatives for slope ($m$) and intercept ($b$) using the chain rule.
    - Understanding the Gradient Descent update rule and the role of the learning rate ($\alpha$).
    - Why iterative methods are necessary for large models (e.g., LLMs).
- **Extra Reading: Analytical Methods & Limitations** * The Least Squares approach and Normal Equations in matrix form.
    - Computational complexity ($O(n^3)$) and why exact solutions fail for massive, non-linear neural networks.
